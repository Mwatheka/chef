Octopus Curry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg octopus, cleaned and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add the octopus pieces and cook for about 30-40 minutes until tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a separate large skillet or pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger to the skillet. Saut√© until the onions become translucent and the mixture is fragrant.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes to the skillet and cook until they soften.
  5. Add the curry powder, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric, and cayenne pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine the spices with the onion and tomato mixture.
  6. Pour in the coconut milk and water. Stir everything together and bring the mixture to a simmer.
  7. Add the cooked octopus pieces to the skillet and stir to coat them with the curry sauce. Simmer for another 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  8. Taste and season with salt as desired.
  9. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.
  10. Serve the Octopus Curry hot with steamed rice or naan bread.

Enjoy your flavorful Octopus Curry!
