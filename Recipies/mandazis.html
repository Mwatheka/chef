Homemade Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom (optional)
  • 1/4 cup coconut milk (or regular milk)
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter or vegetable oil
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix together all-purpose flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and ground cardamom (if using) in a large bowl.
  2. Add coconut milk (or regular milk), water, and melted butter or vegetable oil to the dry ingredients. Stir until a soft dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Cover the dough with a kitchen towel and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  5. Divide the dough into smaller portions and roll each portion into a ball.
  6. Heat oil in a deep pan or skillet for frying (around 350°F or 175°C).
  7. Flatten each dough ball into a circular shape, about 1/4 to 1/2 inch thick.
  8. Carefully place the flattened dough into the hot oil and fry until golden brown on both sides (around 2-3 minutes per side).
  9. Remove the cooked mandazi from the oil and drain excess oil on a paper towel.
  10. Repeat the frying process with the remaining dough.
