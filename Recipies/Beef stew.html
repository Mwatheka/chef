Beef Stew Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 kg beef stew meat, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 stalks of celery, sliced
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot. Saut√© until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
  3. Add the beef stew meat to the pot and cook until it is browned on all sides.
  4. Add the sliced carrots, diced potatoes, and sliced celery to the pot. Stir well to combine.
  5. Pour in the beef broth, diced tomatoes (with their juices), and tomato paste. Stir to incorporate the ingredients.
  6. Add the Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, paprika, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir to combine.
  7. Bring the stew to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot and let the stew simmer for about 2 hours, or until the beef is tender and the flavors are well developed. Stir occasionally.
  8. Taste and adjust the seasonings as needed.
  9. Garnish the Beef Stew with freshly chopped parsley before serving.
  10. Serve the stew hot with crusty bread or rice.

Enjoy your hearty Beef Stew with flavorful spices!
