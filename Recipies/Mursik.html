Mursik Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter fresh cow's milk
  • 1 liter boiling water
  • 2-3 handfuls of burnt cow's dung
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Pour the fresh cow's milk into a clean gourd or calabash.
  2. Add the boiling water to the gourd and let it sit for about 10 minutes to heat the gourd.
  3. Empty the gourd, then add the burnt cow's dung to the gourd. Use a stick to mix and crush the dung, extracting its flavor and color.
  4. Pour the milk back into the gourd and mix it with the dung thoroughly.
  5. Let the mixture sit for about 2-3 hours, allowing the milk to ferment and develop a tangy flavor.
  6. After fermentation, strain the milk through a sieve or strainer to remove any solid particles.
  7. Add salt to taste and stir well.
  8. Serve the Mursik in traditional gourds or calabashes.
