Creamed Spinach Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound fresh spinach
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash the spinach thoroughly to remove any dirt or grit. Discard any tough stems.
  2. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add the spinach and blanch for 1-2 minutes until wilted.
  3. Drain the spinach and rinse with cold water to stop the cooking process. Squeeze out any excess water and chop the spinach.
  4. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
  5. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Saut√© until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
  6. Add the chopped spinach to the skillet and stir to combine with the onion and garlic.
  7. Pour in the heavy cream and sprinkle the ground nutmeg over the spinach. Stir well to coat the spinach with the cream.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the cream thickens and coats the spinach.
  9. Remove from heat and serve hot as a side dish.

Enjoy your delicious Creamed Spinach!
