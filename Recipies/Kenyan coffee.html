Coffee Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons Kenyan coffee beans
  • 2 cups water
  • Sugar or sweetener (optional)
  • Milk or cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Grind the Kenyan coffee beans to a medium-coarse consistency.
  2. In a saucepan, bring the water to a boil.
  3. Add the ground coffee to the boiling water and stir well.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and let the coffee simmer for about 2-3 minutes.
  5. Remove the saucepan from heat and let it sit for a minute to allow the coffee grounds to settle at the bottom.
  6. Carefully pour the brewed coffee into cups, leaving the settled grounds behind in the saucepan.
  7. If desired, add sugar or sweetener to taste, and stir well until dissolved.
  8. Optional: Add milk or cream to taste.
  9. Serve the Kenyan Coffee hot and enjoy!
