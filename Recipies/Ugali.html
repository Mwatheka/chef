Ugali recipie

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of maize flour
  • 4 cups of water
  • Pinch of salt (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large saucepan, bring the water to a boil.
  2. Slowly add the maize flour into the boiling water, stirring continuously to prevent lumps.
  3. Continue stirring vigorously to mix the flour and water thoroughly.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and cover the saucepan with a lid.
  5. Allow the mixture to simmer for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  6. Once the mixture thickens and forms a stiff consistency, remove from heat.
  7. Transfer the Ugali onto a serving dish or mold it into individual portions.
  8. Serve hot with your choice of side dish, such as sukuma wiki, stew, or grilled meat.

Enjoy your delicious Ugali!
