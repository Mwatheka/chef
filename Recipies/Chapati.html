Chapati Recipie

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1.5 cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and salt.
  2. Add the warm water gradually while kneading the dough with your hands. Continue kneading until you have a smooth and elastic dough. If the dough is too sticky, add a little more flour; if it's too dry, add a little more water.
  3. Drizzle the vegetable oil over the dough and knead again for a few minutes to incorporate the oil.
  4. Divide the dough into small golf ball-sized portions. Roll each portion into a smooth ball using your hands.
  5. On a lightly floured surface, take one dough ball and roll it out into a thin, round chapati using a rolling pin. Aim for a thickness of about 1/8 inch.
  6. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat. Place the rolled chapati on the heated skillet and cook for about 1-2 minutes, or until small bubbles start to form on the surface.
  7. Flip the chapati and cook the other side for another 1-2 minutes until lightly browned and cooked through. Press down gently with a spatula to help it cook evenly.
  8. Remove the cooked chapati from the skillet and place it on a plate. Repeat the process with the remaining dough balls.
  9. Serve the hot and freshly cooked Chapatis with your favorite curry or dish.

Enjoy your delicious homemade Chapatis!
