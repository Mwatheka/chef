Fresh Passion Juice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 6 ripe passion fruits
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (optional)
  • 2 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Wash the passion fruits thoroughly.
  2. Cut each passion fruit in half and scoop out the pulp and seeds into a blender or food processor.
  3. Add sugar (if desired) and cold water to the blender.
  4. Blend the ingredients on high speed until the mixture becomes smooth.
  5. Place a fine-mesh sieve over a pitcher and pour the blended mixture through the sieve to remove any pulp or seeds.
  6. Use a spoon to press down on the pulp and extract all the juice.
  7. If desired, add ice cubes to the pitcher and stir well.
  8. Pour the fresh passion juice into glasses and serve immediately.

Enjoy your refreshing Fresh Passion Juice!
