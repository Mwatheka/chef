Mixeed Tea (Chai ya Maziwa)

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons loose black tea leaves
  • 2-3 cardamom pods, crushed
  • 1 small piece of cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 1 small piece of ginger, grated
  • Sugar to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, bring water to a boil.
  2. Add the crushed cardamom pods, cinnamon stick, cloves, and grated ginger to the boiling water. Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes to infuse the flavors.
  3. Add the loose black tea leaves to the pot and let it brew for a minute or two, depending on how strong you like your tea.
  4. Pour in the milk and bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Remove the pot from heat and strain the tea into cups to remove the tea leaves and spices.
  6. Add sugar to taste and stir well until the sugar is dissolved.
  7. Serve the Mixed Tea with Masala hot and enjoy!
