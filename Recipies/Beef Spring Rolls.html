Beef Spring Rolls

Ingredients:

  • 8 spring roll wrappers
  • 200g ground beef
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Oil for frying
  • Sweet chili sauce or dipping sauce of your choice (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet or wok, heat a tablespoon of oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped onion, and saut√© until fragrant and translucent.
  2. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through. Break up any lumps with a spatula.
  3. Add the shredded cabbage, carrots, and bean sprouts to the skillet. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly softened.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, and black pepper. Pour the sauce over the beef and vegetable mixture in the skillet. Stir well to combine and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  5. Remove the skillet from the heat and let the filling cool completely.
  6. Take one spring roll wrapper and place it on a clean surface in a diamond shape, with one corner facing you.
  7. Spoon a portion of the beef and vegetable filling onto the bottom corner of the wrapper, leaving some space on the sides. Fold the bottom corner over the filling, then fold the sides inward. Roll up tightly towards the remaining corner, sealing the edges with a bit of water.
  8. Repeat the process with the remaining spring roll wrappers and filling.
  9. In a deep frying pan or wok, heat enough oil for deep frying over medium-high heat.
  10. Carefully place the spring rolls into the hot oil, a few at a time, and fry until they turn golden brown and crispy. Remove them with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  11. Serve the Beef Spring Rolls hot with sweet chili sauce or your preferred dipping sauce.
