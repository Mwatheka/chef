Vegetable and Lentil Soup Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups chopped vegetables (such as zucchini, bell peppers, and spinach)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the lentils under cold water and set aside.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, and celery, and saut√© until they begin to soften, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic, cumin, coriander, and paprika to the pot, and cook for an additional minute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the vegetable broth and diced tomatoes, including their juices. Stir in the lentils and bring the mixture to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat to low and let the soup simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  6. Add the chopped vegetables to the pot and cook for another 10 minutes, or until the vegetables are cooked to your desired tenderness.
  7. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley or cilantro.
