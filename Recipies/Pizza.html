Homemade Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 and 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 and 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Pizza sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions)

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, instant yeast, sugar, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the warm water and olive oil.
  3. Stir the mixture with a wooden spoon until a dough forms.
  4. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface and knead it for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.
  6. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  7. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release the air. Transfer it to a lightly floured surface and divide it into desired portions (e.g., two for personal-sized pizzas).
  8. Roll out each portion of dough into a round shape to your desired thickness.
  9. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  10. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  11. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  12. Add your desired toppings, such as pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, or any other ingredients you prefer.
  13. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  14. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
