Homemade Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional toppings: fried onions, raisins, cashews

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add the minced garlic, grated ginger, cumin seeds, ground coriander, ground turmeric, and cinnamon stick to the pot. Cook for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the rinsed rice to the pot and stir well to coat it with the spices. Cook for another minute.
  5. Pour in the vegetable or chicken broth and add salt to taste. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  6. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the rice simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed. Avoid lifting the lid during this time.
  7. Remove the pot from heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes. Then fluff the rice with a fork.
  8. Serve the pilau hot and garnish with fried onions, raisins, or cashews if desired.
