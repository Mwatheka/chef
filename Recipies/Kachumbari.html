Kachumbari

Ingredients:

  • 3 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cucumber, finely chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chopped tomatoes, red onion, cucumber, green bell pepper, and cilantro leaves.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the vegetable mixture in the large bowl.
  4. Gently toss all the ingredients together until well combined and evenly coated with the dressing.
  5. Let the Kachumbari sit for about 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Give it a final toss before serving.
  7. Serve the Kachumbari as a side dish or condiment with grilled meat, fish, or as a topping for chapati or rice.

Enjoy your refreshing Kachumbari!
