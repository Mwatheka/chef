Grilled Fish with Coconut Sauce Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 fish fillets (tilapia, snapper, or any white fish)
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the lemon juice, paprika, turmeric, ground cumin, salt, and pepper to make a marinade.
  3. Pat dry the fish fillets and brush them with the marinade on both sides.
  4. Grill the fish fillets for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they are cooked through and have nice grill marks. Set them aside.
  5. In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  6. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and sliced red bell pepper to the skillet. Saut√© until the vegetables are tender.
  7. Add the curry powder and cook for another minute, stirring constantly.
  8. Pour in the coconut milk and soy sauce. Stir well to combine the ingredients.
  9. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for about 5-7 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  10. Place the grilled fish fillets in the skillet with the coconut sauce. Spoon some of the sauce over the fish.
  11. Cover the skillet and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat the fish through.
  12. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro leaves.
  13. Serve the grilled fish with coconut sauce alongside steamed rice or naan bread.

Enjoy your delicious Grilled Fish with Coconut Sauce!
