Melon Juice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe melon (cantaloupe or honeydew)
  • 2 tablespoons honey (optional)
  • 1 cup cold water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Wash the melon thoroughly, then cut it in half and remove the seeds.
  2. Using a spoon, scoop out the melon flesh and place it in a blender or food processor.
  3. Add the honey (if desired) and cold water to the blender.
  4. Blend the ingredients until smooth and well combined.
  5. If desired, add a few ice cubes to the blender and blend again to make the juice chilled.
  6. Pour the melon juice into glasses and serve immediately.
  7. You can garnish with a slice of melon or mint leaves, if desired.

Enjoy your refreshing Melon Juice!
