Vegetable Samosa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and salt. Add vegetable oil and mix well to form a crumbly texture.
  2. Add water gradually and knead to form a smooth dough. Cover the dough and let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, heat a tablespoon of oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add minced garlic and grated ginger. Saut√© for a minute until fragrant.
  5. Add chopped onions and cook until they turn translucent.
  6. Add chopped carrots, green peas, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and red chili powder (if using). Mix well and cook for a few minutes.
  7. Add mashed potatoes and salt. Mix everything together and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Turn off the heat and add chopped fresh cilantro. Mix well and let the filling cool.
  9. Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into a thin, circular shape.
  10. Cut the circular dough into halves, creating two semi-circles.
  11. Take one semi-circle, wet the straight edge with water, and form a cone by bringing the two ends together. Press the edges to seal.
  12. Fill the cone with the prepared vegetable filling and seal the top by pressing the edges firmly.
  13. Repeat the process with the remaining dough and filling.
  14. Heat oil for frying in a deep pan or skillet over medium heat.
  15. Carefully place the samosas into the hot oil, a few at a time, and fry until they turn golden brown and crispy. Remove them with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  16. Serve the Vegetable Samosas hot with chutney or sauce of your choice.
