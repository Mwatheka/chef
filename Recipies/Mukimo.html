Mukimo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups maize (corn), kernels removed from the cob
  • 2 cups pumpkin leaves or kale, finely chopped
  • 2 cups green peas
  • 2 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 medium-sized onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water for boiling

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add the maize kernels, pumpkin leaves or kale, green peas, and diced potatoes. Cook until all the ingredients are tender.
  2. In a separate pan, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, sauté until the onion becomes translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
  3. Add the sautéed onion and garlic mixture to the cooked ingredients in the pot.
  4. Mash the mixture using a wooden spoon or a potato masher until everything is well combined and mashed together. The consistency should be thick and slightly chunky.
  5. Season with salt to taste and mix well.
  6. Simmer the mixture for a few more minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot.

Enjoy your flavorful Mukimo dish!
