Kebab Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef or lamb
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Skewers

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef or lamb, chopped onion, minced garlic, parsley, cumin, coriander, paprika, cinnamon, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to evenly distribute the spices.
  2. Cover the bowl and refrigerate the mixture for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld together.
  3. Preheat your grill or stovetop grill pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Divide the meat mixture into equal portions and shape them into long, cylindrical kebabs around the skewers.
  5. Brush the kebabs with olive oil to prevent sticking and enhance flavor.
  6. Place the kebabs on the preheated grill and cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are browned and cooked through.
  7. Remove the kebabs from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.
  8. Serve the Kebabs hot with pita bread, rice, salad, and your favorite sauce or dip.
