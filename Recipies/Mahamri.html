Mahamri Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter or ghee
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and ground cardamom. Mix well.
  2. Add the coconut milk and warm water to the dry ingredients. Knead the dough until it becomes soft and pliable.
  3. Cover the dough with a clean kitchen towel and let it rest for about 1 hour to allow it to rise.
  4. After the resting period, divide the dough into small portions and shape them into round discs or triangles.
  5. In a deep frying pan, heat the oil over medium heat.
  6. Gently place the shaped dough into the hot oil and fry until golden brown on both sides. Flip them occasionally for even cooking.
  7. Once cooked, remove the mahamri from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  8. Serve the warm mahamri with tea or your favorite dipping sauce.

Enjoy your delicious Mahamri!
