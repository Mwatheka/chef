Hot Dogs

Ingredients:

  • 4 hot dog buns
  • 4 hot dogs
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup yellow mustard
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup pickle relish
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sauerkraut, jalapenos, etc.

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a grill or stovetop griddle over medium heat.
  2. Place the hot dogs on the grill or griddle and cook, turning occasionally, until they are heated through and nicely browned.
  3. While the hot dogs are cooking, prepare the toppings by placing the diced onions and pickle relish in separate bowls.
  4. Warm the hot dog buns on the grill or griddle for a minute or two until they are slightly toasted.
  5. Remove the hot dogs from the heat and place them into the warm buns.
  6. Add your desired condiments and toppings. Common choices include ketchup, mustard, diced onions, and pickle relish.
  7. Feel free to get creative with additional toppings like shredded cheese, sauerkraut, jalapenos, or any other toppings of your choice.
  8. Serve the Hot Dogs immediately and enjoy!
