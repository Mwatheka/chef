Mango Juice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe mangoes
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (optional)
  • 2 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Wash the mangoes and remove the skin and pit.
  2. Cut the mango flesh into chunks and place them in a blender or food processor.
  3. Add sugar (if desired) and cold water to the blender.
  4. Blend the ingredients on high speed until the mixture becomes smooth and creamy.
  5. Place a fine-mesh sieve over a pitcher and pour the blended mixture through the sieve to remove any fibrous strands or chunks.
  6. Use a spoon to press down on the pulp and extract all the juice.
  7. If desired, add ice cubes to the pitcher and stir well.
  8. Pour the mango juice into glasses and serve immediately.

Enjoy your refreshing Mango Juice!
