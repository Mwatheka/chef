Sukuma Wiki Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch of sukuma wiki (collard greens or kale)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Wash the sukuma wiki thoroughly and remove any tough stems. Chop the leaves into small pieces.
  2. In a large pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pan. Saut√© until the onion becomes translucent.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes, cumin powder, paprika, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine the spices with the onion and garlic mixture.
  5. Add the chopped sukuma wiki to the pan and stir to coat it evenly with the spice mixture.
  6. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and let the sukuma wiki cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until the greens are tender.
  7. If the pan becomes too dry during cooking, add a small amount of water to prevent burning and to create some steam.
  8. Once the sukuma wiki is cooked to your desired tenderness, remove from heat.
  9. Serve hot as a side dish with your choice of Ugali or rice.

Enjoy your flavorful Sukuma Wiki!
