Nyama Choma

Ingredients:

  • 2 kg beef or goat meat, cut into large chunks
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • Juice of 2 lemons

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the minced garlic, vegetable oil, paprika, ground cumin, black pepper, salt, and lemon juice to make a marinade.
  2. Add the meat chunks to the marinade and toss well to coat them evenly. Cover the bowl and let the meat marinate in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours or overnight for the best flavor.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Remove the meat from the marinade and let any excess marinade drip off.
  5. Grill the meat on the preheated grill, turning occasionally, until it is nicely charred and cooked to your desired level of doneness. This may take around 10-15 minutes depending on the thickness of the meat.
  6. Once cooked, remove the meat from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes.
  7. Slice the Nyama Choma into smaller pieces and serve it hot with Kachumbari (tomato and onion salad) and your favorite dipping sauce.

Enjoy your delicious Nyama Choma!
