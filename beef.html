Beef Fry Marinated with Honey Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound (450g) beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Chopped green onions or cilantro for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the soy sauce, honey, Worcestershire sauce, minced garlic, minced ginger, black pepper, and salt. Stir well until the honey is dissolved and the marinade is well combined.
  2. Add the thinly sliced beef to the marinade, making sure each slice is coated evenly. You can use your hands or a spoon to mix and coat the beef. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes. For best results, marinate it for a few hours or overnight.
  3. Heat a large skillet or frying pan over medium-high heat. Add the vegetable oil and let it heat up.
  4. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated beef to the skillet, spreading it out in a single layer. You may need to cook the beef in batches, depending on the size of your pan. Reserve the remaining marinade.
  5. Allow the beef to cook for 2-3 minutes on one side until it develops a brown crust. Flip the beef slices and cook for another 2-3 minutes on the other side.
  6. Reduce the heat to medium-low and pour the reserved marinade over the beef in the skillet. Stir everything together and let it cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the sauce to thicken slightly.
  7. Remove the skillet from heat and transfer the beef fry to a serving dish. Garnish with chopped green onions or cilantro if desired.
  8. Serve the beef fry hot as a main dish with steamed rice or as a filling for tacos, wraps, or sandwiches.
